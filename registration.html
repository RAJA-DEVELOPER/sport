Medical Readiness

Safety is our priority. Every athlete must meet the following medical standards before full-contact training.

Physical Clearance

A signed fitness certificate from a registered pediatrician or GP is mandatory for students under 18.

Blood Grouping

Accurate blood group information must be provided for our emergency medical database during enrollment.

Allergy Disclosure

Any known allergies (food, environmental, or medical) must be declared to the head coach and campus medic.
