Weather Policy

In the event of heavy rain or thunderstorms, all outdoor training sessions will automatically migrate to the theoretical video-analysis room or the indoor physical conditioning gym. Timings remain identical.

Upcoming Holidays

The academy outdoor pitches will be entirely closed for annual maintenance from 22nd December to 2nd January. Gyms and indoor tennis courts remain open for Pro-level members.

Annual Training Roadmap

We follow a structured periodization model to ensure peak performance for all athletes.

Pre-Season

Jan - Mar

Focus on foundational strength, endurance, and basic technical drills.

In-Season

Apr - Sep

Advanced tactics, match practice, and league participation.

Post-Season

Oct - Nov

Recovery, biomechanical analysis, and specialized skill workshops.

Off-Season

December

Rest, recuperation, and light recreational sports activities.

Attendance Policy
